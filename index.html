FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions before you apply.

What makes your coaching different?

Most trading education teaches you what to think. I teach you how to think: reading price action, building context, managing risk, and developing a repeatable decision-making process. The goal is independence, not dependence on me.

Do I need trading experience?

No. Beginners have the advantage of learning the right way from day one without unlearning bad habits. Experienced traders usually just need a structured process, better feedback, and a clearer way to think about the market.

How long until I'm trading real money?

Some clients go live within a couple of months, others take longer. Trading is a marathon, not a sprint. You're ready when you're ready, and the process will tell us both when that is.

Can't I just learn this on YouTube?

Possibly. Most of the information is out there. The challenge is knowing what's worth learning, in what order, and how to apply it consistently in a live market. Like baseball, high-level skill needs structured practice, expert feedback, and a proven process.
